Title: Una San Luis

----

Text: Una San Luis. A crisp and crushable Mexican blonde lager first brewed to quench the thirst of the hard working mining community of San Luis. Balanced, light body, and slightly sweet coming from a mix of pilsner malt and maiz grain. 100% natural ingredients.
